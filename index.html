The dawn of crypto-native non-profits

We believe that blockchain technology is changing how we align interests and resources behind global causes, such as biodiversity protection. Rewilder will be crypto-native:

  • 100% of funds raised in crypto, and kept in crypto (multisig contract). No bank account.
  • Endowment fund to safeguard value of donations and maintenance costs will be 100% on-chain and invest on-chain only in low-risk DeFi projects.
  • We’ll try our best to pay for the land purchase in crypto.
  • Legacy world non-profit entity (501c3 based in Miami, Florida) will act as a proxy to the traditional legal system (to own the lands and keys to funds).
  • Fund transparency tool allows donors to track exactly how money is used.

FAQ

Why are you doing this?

We’re all kind of scared that we might not make it as a species past this century, given the rate at which we’re destroying our home planet. As Humanity creates the technology to expand life in space, we need to slow down the destruction of Earth. Nation states are not the right technology to solve global problems. We should stop counting on slow paced, fragmented bureaucracies to coordinate policies more efficiently at a global scale. Blockchain is a great technology to quickly coordinate people globally and have transparency on the use of funds. More info on the motivation here.

Who is this for?

If you’d like to contribute to preserving the wild side of Earth and delaying some of the negative effects of human growth on the biosphere... If you think nonprofits and charities should level up 10x their transparency and tech stack… If you thought about buying land for conservation on your own and would like to team up… If you made wild gains with your crypto investments lately and would like to give back to nature… Rewilder is for you!

How much can I donate to make an impact?

Each donation creates a unique NFT. All funds raised will be pooled together to purchase land for rewilding at the best rates we can find. Once the real estate is owned by our recently established foundation, we’ll virtually divide the property according to share of the total money contributed, and assign a section of the land to each NFT. Our current best estimates say that donating the equivalent of $1000 in crypto will end up contributing ~X hectares (~Y acres) of wild land to the project.

How will you choose where to buy land?

We’re working with biodiversity conservation experts to assess the biggest rewilding opportunities worldwide. Given this is only our first experiment, we want to take it slow and go with a safe bet. We want this to grow organically and build a community around it. Future rewilder fundraising efforts will probably have locations pre-determined.

How will the funds be used?

All fund movements will be on-chain, and we’ll build a transparency tool so that donors can account for every cent spent. We’ll strive to use the vast majority of funds just to purchase land. However, a small % of the money donated will be kept in a crypto endowment fund to pay for basic recurring costs (ranger/security payroll, taxes and other maintenance fees). None of the people currently working on the project are receiving or will receive any monetary compensation. The project setup costs were covered via private donations.

  • No management fees.
  • No conservationists will be hired.
  • No wildlife species reintroduction.
  • No active projects of any kind, just passive protection.
  • No bank account fees.
  • No lawyer fees.
  • Purchase of land for passive rewilding.
  • Invest in low risk DeFi for endowment.
  • Pay taxes and security fees.
